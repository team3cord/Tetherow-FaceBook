Crescent Neighborhood

  • HOMESITES: 42
  • AVAILABLE SITES: 7
  • PRICE RANGE: $225,000-$300,000
  • ACREAGE RANGE: 0.43-0.71

Natural, park-like, sanctuary — these are all descriptors that have been used by people who live in, or who have visited, the Crescent neighborhood in Tetherow. Set on the divide that runs between the back edge of the Broken Top and Tetherow golf courses, this neighborhood offers some impressive homes. Large, mature pines frame panoramic vistas of the mountains, and deep setbacks offer privacy and opportunities for intimate outdoor spaces. Spacious lots allow future residents to easily design the home of their dreams. Come and see why only a handful of these desirable home sites remain.

Glen Neighborhood

  • HOMESITES: 68
  • AVAILABLE SITES: 37
  • PRICE RANGE: $170,000-$335,000
  • ACREAGE RANGE: 0.3-0.66

If you are looking for a charming neighborhood with a real sense of community, the Glen should be on your list. This collection of homesites offers both privacy and immediate access to open spaces, and lends itself more to adventure than hours of yard work. Enjoy direct access to the hiking trails (great for any age!), mountain bike parks and world-class mountain bike trails in the Deschutes National Park Trail system. With more than 15 homes built here already, this neighborhood offers a sense of community with opportunity for growth and adventure!

Heath Neighborhood

  • HOMESITES: 156
  • AVAILABLE SITES: 1
  • PRICE RANGE: $107,630-$269,120
  • ACREAGE RANGE: 0.31-1.51

If ever there was a location to build a retreat where the separation between outdoors and indoors is blurred, Heath is that location. Almost everywhere you look, the views demand attention. Choose between a lot situated on the bluff facing a panoramic scene of the snow-covered Cascades, or a lot where both your house and the links-style course of Tetherow are seamlessly tucked into the landscape – or a combination of both. This enclave is situated in the heart of Tetherow, naturally secluded and immediately accessible.
